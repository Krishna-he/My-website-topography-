What is Team India

The India men's national cricket team represents India in men's international cricket. It is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), and is a Full Member of the International Cricket Council (ICC) with Test, One Day International (ODI) and Twenty20 International (T20I) status.

ICC-The International Cricket Council (ICC) is the global governing body for international cricket, encompassing men's, women's and youth participation and competition. It is governed by the ICC Constitution.

ODI-One Day International (ODI) is a form of limited overs cricket played between two teams with international status. Each team faces a fixed number of overs, currently 50, with the game lasting up to 7 hours. The Cricket World Cup is played in this format12. ODI matches are typically used to decide the winner in a day
